About DEED

Intelligence for the Future

Buying or selling a home is an emotional journey filled with hopes, fears, and dreams. It is the largest financial decision most people ever make — and for decades the platforms built to help have hidden the most important information about long-term value.

DEED does two things no other platform does. First — we layer real-time insurance carrier withdrawal data on every property in America, listed or not. When carriers withdraw from a county they send the most accurate climate risk signal available, months before any public record reflects it. We aggregate that signal across all 50 states using AI.

Second — because your bank pays insurance through escrow, the real monthly payment includes the real insurance cost. Zillow shows you a standard estimate. In insurance-stressed markets that estimate can be $1,000+ per month wrong. DEED shows you the true PITI before you fall in love with a property — not at closing.

Our price estimates are built on insurance viability and climate risk data — not just recent comps. Our estimate and Zillow's will often be different numbers. That difference is the most honest conversation in real estate.
